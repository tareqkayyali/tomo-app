1. Who we are

Tomo is an AI coaching app for young athletes. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it.

2. Who can use Tomo

Tomo is available to athletes aged 13 and older. If you are under 16 and live in the European Union or the United Kingdom, you must have a parent or legal guardian accept this Privacy Policy on your behalf before your account is activated.

3. What we collect

  • Account information — name, email, date of birth.
  • Athletic profile — sport, position, goal, height, weight.
  • Wellness and training data — daily check-ins (energy, soreness, sleep, mood, academic stress), training sessions you log, test results.
  • Conversations with Tomo — messages you exchange with the AI coach.
  • Device data — basic diagnostics (crash reports, app version) that do not identify you personally.

4. Why we collect it

To personalise your coaching, monitor your readiness and load, flag safety risks (such as training through pain or during a growth spurt), and improve the product. We do not sell personal data.

5. Analytics and advertising

We do not show advertising. Analytics (anonymised product usage) is off by default for users under 16 and can be turned on or off at any time in Settings.

6. How long we keep your data

Wellness and training data is retained for up to 24 months from the date of each entry. Conversations with Tomo are retained for up to 6 months. You can extend or shorten these defaults in Settings. When you delete your account, all associated data is removed within 30 days.

7. Parental rights

Parents who have consented on behalf of a minor can view their child's activity summary, revoke consent, or request deletion at any time from the parent portal. Revoking consent immediately stops new data collection.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR, UK GDPR, and applicable US state laws you can access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact privacy@tomo.app. We respond within 30 days.

9. Where your data lives

Data is processed on infrastructure provided by Supabase and Railway. Some processing (AI responses) happens with Anthropic and Voyage AI. All processors are bound by data-processing agreements that prohibit retention beyond what is necessary to serve your request.

10. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: privacy@tomo.app.
