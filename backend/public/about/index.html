What is Tomo?

Tomo is an AI-powered wellness and performance app built for young athletes ages 13 to 23. Whether you play football, basketball, tennis, or padel, Tomo meets you where you are.

Every day, a quick 15-second check-in captures how you feel — energy, soreness, sleep, mood. Tomo turns that into a readiness score that tells you whether to push hard, go light, or rest.

Your training plan adapts in real time. No more guessing. No more overtraining. Just smart, safe coaching that keeps you on the pitch and off the bench.

Safety Always Comes First

Tomo will never tell you to push through pain. If something hurts, the recommendation is always rest and recovery. Your long-term health matters more than any single training session.

Tomo is not medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for injuries or health concerns.
